Score System Rules – Survivors

General

  • Escape: +10 rating points
  • +10 additional points for each teammate who escapes
  • Every 25% of generator repair grants +2.5 points to the repairing survivor
  • Every 25% of healing progress grants +1 point
  • Saving a survivor while the Killer is attempting to pick them up: +10
  • Saving a survivor from the Killer’s shoulder: +5
    This bonus is granted when the survivor escapes using the "Breakout" perk by the perk owner. It is also granted when using a Saboteur perk or toolbox to break a hook, but in this case points are awarded only if both the Killer and the rescuer were within 4 meters of the sabotaged hook at the moment of escape.
  • Killer stun: +1
  • Killer hinder (slowdown): +1
  • Unhook: +2.5

Runner

  • Earns bonus rating points for chase performance
  • Against standard M1 Killers:
    • +2 points per 10s of chase while healthy
    • +3 points per 10s while injured (+2 with "Iron Will" perk)
  • Against Killers that apply easy first hits:
    • +3 points per 10s healthy
    • +2 points per 10s injured
  • Against high-pressure Killers:
    • +3 points per 10s regardless of health state
  • Losing a chase: +3
  • Stun / blind during chase: +2
  • Hinder during chase: +2

Altruist

  • Stun or blind the Killer: +4
  • Carry saves grant double points
  • Hook saves grant double points
  • Basement saves grant quadruple points
  • Endurance activation from Killer hit:
    • +10 normally
    • +15 when triggered through "Mettle of Man" perk
  • Protection hit: +5 (Works only if the other survivor is within 5 meters of the killer

Medic

  • 25% heal: +2.5
  • Full heal state: +10
  • Healing Runner or Altruist: +5 per 25%
  • Pick up survivor from dying state: +10
  • Pick up Runner: +15

Engineer

  • Breaking a 3-gen formation grants +5 for 25%
  • Repairing within 7m of the Killer: +5 per 25%
  • Opening exit gates: +5
  • Opening exit gates within 7m of the Killer: +10

Ranked Mode

Ranked mode is built around strategic decision-making from both sides – from thoughtful build choices to tactics and moment-to-moment actions.

Survivors must become a true team, working in perfect coordination to achieve victory – while the Killer must rise as a relentless threat.

In Ranked Mode, Survivors can choose from 4 distinct roles, each providing unique advantages. Choose wisely, based on your playstyle and strengths.

Enjoy your suffering in the Fog.

Gameplay

At the beginning of a match, both sides block 3 perks for their opponents – one from each category. Survivors restrict Hex, Generator Regression and Blocking perks, while the Killer blocks Exhaustion, Boon and Second Chance perks.

After that, both sides have 60 seconds to finalize their builds.

The match then proceeds to the map veto phase – each side alternately bans 4 realms and then selects 2 from the remaining ones. One realm is chosen at random from the 4 selected, along with a random variation if available.

During the trial, both sides earn rating points based on their performance and in-game actions.

The final outcome is determined by comparing the Killer’s total rating points to the total rating points of all Survivors.

More details can be found in the Survivor / Killer rule sections.
There are 6 ranks in total – five of them are divided into subranks, from IV to I.

Each subrank requires 100 rating points to progress.

The sixth rank stands above the rest – it is not just simply progression, but the ultimate goal – to become the perfect servant of the Entity.
